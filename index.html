How do I add movies or TV shows to my watchlist?

Use the search bar at the top to find titles from TMDb. Click "Add to watchlist" on any search result card to save it.

How do I add items to Collections?

Add custom tags to any item in the detail modal. Tags act as collection names‚Äîitems with the same tag will appear together in the Collections view. For example, adding the tag "animation" to multiple items creates an "animation" collection.

How do I organize my watchlist?

Use the status dropdown (Watch later, Watching, On hold, Finished) in each item's detail modal. Add tags for custom organization, and drag items to reorder them.

How do I sync my watchlist across devices?

Sign in with Google using the "Sign in" button in the top right. Your watchlist will automatically sync to Firebase and be available on any device where you're signed in.

Where can I watch the movies/shows?

Click any item to open its detail modal. The "Watch" button shows available streaming services. Click it to open the provider's page for that title.

How do I mark something as finished?

Open the item's detail modal, change the status to "Finished", and optionally set a finished date. The item will appear in your "Finished" filter.
